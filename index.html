"Hyde Memorial State park"

Hyde memorial state park is the oldest state park in new mexico it was founded in 1938, it is located at the base of the sangre de cristo mountain range in new mexicos capital city santa fe, which is also the oldest state capital in the united states

"City of Rocks State park"

City of rocks state park was founded in the year 1953, it is a large open park thet has tall round and flat rock formation that can stand up to 40ft tall.

"Pancho Villa State Park"

Pancho villa state park is located less then 5 miles from the United States and Mexico border, The state park is named after famed spanish leader pancho villa who the state park is named after, he conquerd the city in 1916 and put it under spanish control.

"Oasis State Park"

Oasis state park is located 5 miles north of portales, NM. it was founded in the year 1961 and is know for the samll lake in the park that is good for fising and swimming.

"Sugarite State Park"

Sugarite state park, is the only new mexico state park that extends into colorado, it is nesseld along the base of the rockies and is about 20 miles north of raton,NM. it was founded in the year 1966

"Rockhound State Park"

Rockhound state park was founded in the year 1965, and is know for its large anmound of mineral deposits, you can find such gems as quartz and geodes as well as many others, the nearst city is Demming, NM

"Villanueva State Park"

Villanueva state park is named after the old new mexico village that is nearby, it was founded in the year 1967, and sits along the banks of the pecos river.

""Coyote Creek State Park"

Coyote creek state park was founded in 1969 and is home to some of the best special trout waters of new mexico, New mexico game and fish have implimented many rules and regulations to fishing the water here to try and protect the massive trout species.

"Living Desert Zoo and Gardens State Park"

This state park is loacted south of carlsbad in the chihuahuan desert, it shows visitors many desert creaturs and wildlife as well as plants in thier natural habitat

"Leasburg Dam State Park"

leasburg dam state park is located along the rio grande river and is 15 miles south of las cruces, NM. it was founded in the year 1971

"Manzano State Park"

Manzano state park was founded in 1973 and is know for its big game deer and doe hunting, the closest city to the manzano state park is mountainair, NM

"Cimarron Canyon State Park"

Cimarron canyon state park is home to eagles nest lake one of new mexicos biggest lakes, it is know for great camping spots as well as fly fishing. it was founded in the year 1979.

"Oliver Lee State Park"

Oliver lee state park is named after famous new mexico gun fighter Oliver lee who settled here near the end of his life, a ranch house of his still sits on the land of the state park still today.

"Vietnam Memorial state Park"

This state park was named and established in honor of the veterans who served in the war, in 2017 control of the state park was transford to the departmnet of veternan services.

"Cerrillos Hills State Park"

Cerrillos hills state park is new mexicos newest state park it was founded in 2009 and is settled in an old minning valley where you can find hiking paths that take you along old mines and gem sites.
